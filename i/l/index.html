seethe.
breathe through your anger, feel it scrape against your teeth and ruin you.
your anger will burn the leaves to make room for ice.
*
seethe.
unearth every hatchet which lies in the soil of each soul.
they will cleave you too.
*
seethe.
and let it soothe you. never fear:
you have already shown me.
I have already shown you.
I was born in flowers, and now I consume them.

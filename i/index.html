"i"
a poem by tes

nothing in this world matters as much
i feel so much; there is such movement in the feelings i feel
you... immense and endless, so, so much to feel and hold and worship
no matter who I am, no matter what is spat on my face and mashed into my eyes
no matter what; you, endless plane of life, all of you, everywhere,
I LOVE YOU.
and yes, you hate me for whatever reason you choose, and you need only touch me to fear,
i love you.
as much as you attempt to destroy me, i want to believe that essense will remain.
there is no hell deeper than the one you have made for me.
just let me lie, and love.
